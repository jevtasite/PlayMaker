The Film Room

Breaking down the game plan

Q1

What's your typical video turnaround time?

Most highlight reels and promotional videos are delivered within 24-48 hours. For same-day urgent content like game day recaps, we offer rush delivery. Longer-form documentaries or cinematic pieces may take 5-7 days depending on complexity and scope.

Q2

What video formats do you deliver?

All videos are delivered in high-resolution MP4 (H.264) optimized for web and social media. We provide multiple aspect ratios (16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for Stories/Reels, 1:1 for Instagram feed) and can deliver in any format you need (MOV, ProRes, etc.).

Q3

Do you provide raw footage?

Yes! Raw footage and project files can be provided upon request. We typically deliver edited videos, but if you need the source files for future edits or archival purposes, we're happy to include them in your package.

Q4

Can you add music and sound effects?

Absolutely! Every video includes professional sound design with royalty-free music, sound effects, and audio mixing. If you have specific tracks or licensing for certain music, we can incorporate those as well.

Q5

What if I need revisions after delivery?

Every project includes 2 rounds of revisions at no extra cost. If you need changes to pacing, color grading, music, or any other element, just let us know and we'll refine it until it's perfect. Additional revisions can be added if needed.

Q6

What makes your videos stand out?

We don't just edit clips—we craft cinematic narratives. Every video is storyboarded with intentional pacing, color grading, and sound design that captures emotion and energy. Our background in sports storytelling ensures your content isn't just watched—it's felt and remembered.
